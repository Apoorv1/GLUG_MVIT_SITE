ABOUT US

GLUG - GNU/Linux user groups are the means of spreading the idea and philosphy of free software.
GLUG serves as a base to technically empower yourself, interact with professionals,
right from installation to hacking the linux kernel, to web technologies like drupal and
at the same time raising these questions of self reliance .

GLUG MVIT - GNU LINUX USERS GROUP, MVIT is a community
for free software enthusiasts. It promotes, teach and contribute for free software.
Various events, workshops and hackathons related to free software are organised in this community.
Be the part of this wonderful community and work towards bringing the "CHANGE".
